What is Art History?
  • Humanistic field of inquiry that studies visual culture. Science and Humanities.
  • Orginally based in connoisseurship.
  • Modern Art History - deals with placing and understanding art in its social/cultural context while examining and analyzing the formal elements of a work of art.
  • Terms: Western group of cultures thought of as having common traditions. North America, Europe, Mesopotamia (not South America) - ideas/traditions more than geography.
  • Dating: BCE, CE (if nothing after, assume CE)
Why do we study the History of Art?
  • Understanding art is critical to becoming a well rounded human being.
  • Art is a window into human feelings and thoughts over time in various cultures.
  • Art reflects the cultural diversity of various societies while at the same time reflects human commonalities.
What is Art?
  • Mimesis - needs to look like real life
  • Utilitarianism - has a use/purpose
  • Expressionism - focuses on human feelings
  • Modernism - art for arts sake
  • Art a Behavior - visual expression to meet human needs
Functions of Art

  • Telling cultural stories
  • Reinforcing cultural beliefs
  • Meeting spiritual needs
  • Addressing important life events

Talk about Art:
  • Form : How is it made/done - materials, elements and principles of design used
  • Content : What is it aobut that we can tell from looking and based on title
  • Context : How does work fit into cultural context
